Mission

The Harvard College Asian Student Arts Project is a student-run organization that aims to provide the community and resources for Pan-Asian and Asian-American student artists to make, celebrate, and discuss art including (but not limited to) theater, film, dance, music, comedy, writing, entertainment, media, and the visual arts. ASAP aims to celebrate the community, experience, culture, and history of Asian students at Harvard with events such as workshops, open mic nights, group outings, panels, and large- and small-scale productions and performances.

The Asian Student Arts Project will produce at least one artistic project that concerns Asian or Asian-American culture, experiences, history, and/or arts each year.
